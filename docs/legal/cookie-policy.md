# Cookie Policy

**Effective Date:** April 3, 2026
**Last Updated:** April 3, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how mirofishmcp.tech ("the Service"), operated by Joel Libni, uses cookies and similar technologies.

---

## 1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They serve various purposes like keeping you logged in, remembering preferences, and understanding how a site is used.

---

## 2. Cookies We Use

We use only **strictly necessary cookies** required for the Service to function. We do not use advertising, analytics, or tracking cookies.

| Cookie | Purpose | Type | Duration |
|--------|---------|------|----------|
| `session_id` | Keeps you logged into the web dashboard | Essential | Session (cleared on browser close) |
| `csrf_token` | Protects against cross-site request forgery | Essential / Security | Session |
| `auth_token` | Persists login if you choose "remember me" | Essential | 30 days |

---

## 3. Third-Party Cookies

### Cloudflare
Cloudflare, our CDN and security provider, may set the following cookies:

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `__cf_bm` | Bot detection and mitigation | 30 minutes |
| `cf_clearance` | Records that you passed a security challenge | Up to 24 hours |

These cookies are set by Cloudflare and governed by [Cloudflare's cookie policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/cookie-policy/).

### LemonSqueezy
If you visit our billing/checkout pages, LemonSqueezy may set cookies for payment processing. These are governed by [LemonSqueezy's privacy policy](https://www.lemonsqueezy.com/privacy).

---

## 4. Cookies We Do NOT Use

- No analytics cookies (no Google Analytics, Plausible, or similar)
- No advertising or retargeting cookies
- No social media tracking cookies
- No third-party tracking pixels

---

## 5. MCP Client Usage

If you use the Service through an MCP client (Claude Code, Claude Desktop, or similar), no cookies are involved. The MCP protocol communicates via stdio or SSE, authenticated by API key. This Cookie Policy applies only to the web dashboard at mirofishmcp.tech.

---

## 6. Managing Cookies

Since we only use essential cookies, disabling them may prevent the web dashboard from functioning correctly. You can manage cookies through your browser settings:

- **Chrome:** Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
- **Firefox:** Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies
- **Safari:** Preferences > Privacy > Cookies

Disabling cookies does not affect API or MCP access.

---

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy if we add new features that require additional cookies. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date. If we ever introduce non-essential cookies, we will implement a consent mechanism before setting them.
