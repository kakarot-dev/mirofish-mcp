# Acceptable Use Policy

**Effective Date:** April 3, 2026
**Last Updated:** April 3, 2026

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") governs your use of DeepMiro ("the Service"), operated at mirofishmcp.tech by Joel Libni. This AUP is part of our [Terms of Service](/docs/legal/terms-of-service).

---

## 1. Purpose

DeepMiro provides AI-powered swarm prediction simulations. This policy exists to prevent misuse of the Service and to protect our users, infrastructure, and the broader community.

---

## 2. Prohibited Uses

You must not use the Service to:

### Harmful or dangerous content
- Simulate scenarios intended to plan, facilitate, or incite violence, terrorism, or harm to individuals or groups
- Generate content that sexualizes minors in any context
- Create simulations designed to produce instructions for weapons, explosives, or dangerous substances
- Produce disinformation campaigns or coordinated inauthentic behavior

### Fraud and deception
- Generate fake predictions presented as factual or authoritative analysis to deceive others
- Impersonate individuals, organizations, or institutions in simulation outputs
- Use simulation outputs to manipulate financial markets, elections, or public opinion through deception
- Create or distribute outputs intended to defraud

### Harassment and abuse
- Target specific real individuals with simulations intended to harass, stalk, threaten, or defame them
- Generate non-consensual intimate or degrading content about real people
- Use simulations to facilitate discrimination based on race, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, religion, disability, or other protected characteristics

### Legal violations
- Process data you do not have the right to use (stolen data, unauthorized personal data)
- Violate intellectual property rights of third parties
- Use the Service for any activity that violates applicable law in your jurisdiction

### Infrastructure abuse
- Attempt to circumvent rate limits, authentication, or usage quotas
- Deliberately overload the Service with requests designed to degrade performance
- Probe, scan, or test the vulnerability of the Service infrastructure without authorization
- Access other users' data or accounts without authorization

---

## 3. Permitted Uses

For clarity, the following are explicitly permitted:

- **Research and analysis** — using simulations for legitimate research, forecasting, scenario planning, and strategic analysis
- **Creative and speculative scenarios** — exploring hypothetical scenarios, including controversial or sensitive topics, for legitimate research or creative purposes
- **Competitive analysis** — simulating market dynamics, business scenarios, or competitive landscapes
- **Education** — using simulations for teaching, learning, or academic purposes
- **Red-teaming and security research** — testing the Service's own capabilities and limitations (report findings responsibly to us)

We do not police the *topics* of your simulations. We restrict *intent to cause harm*.

---

## 4. Your Responsibilities

- You are solely responsible for how you use simulation outputs
- Simulation outputs are AI-generated predictions, not statements of fact
- You must not present simulation outputs as professional advice (financial, legal, medical, etc.) without appropriate disclaimers
- You must comply with all applicable laws in your jurisdiction

---

## 5. Enforcement

Violations of this AUP may result in:

1. **Warning** — for first-time or minor violations
2. **Temporary suspension** — for repeated or moderate violations
3. **Permanent termination** — for severe violations or continued misconduct after warnings

We will attempt to notify you before or at the time of enforcement action, unless doing so would compromise safety or is legally prohibited.

Enforcement decisions are made at the Operator's discretion. We are not obligated to review simulation content proactively, but we may do so in response to reports or suspicious activity.
